About Us

HIT MOMENTS is an application used to share lovely, fun, happy moments that members of the HIT club can share with each other to preserve moments in life.

  • Help friends share with each other many things around life.
  • On HIT MOMENTS ,there's no worrying about follower count , just add your best friends and family and live in the moment.
  • With HIT MOMENTS , you're able to be real and share photos to the people that matter.

With the desire to create an application to help members of the HIT club increase their ability to interact with each other, help members understand each other better and build a foundation to help the club develop more, more united. Above all, we hope that the product can be known and used more widely not only within the HIT club but also other members within the Hanoi University of Industry and users across the country.

Facts you may have never heard or been told about the process by which we created this product

01 How was the HIT MOMENTS application founded and developed?

HIT MOMENTS was born from the idea of ​​wanting to build an application to help members of the HIT club to use and the product is highly reusable, so HIT MOMENTS is a product that our team founded and built to meet that need.

02 How long has HIT MOMENTS been in development?

HIT MOMENTS was built on June 10th after the development process, the product was first released to everyone on August 11th.

03 What difficulties and challenges did the team encounter during development?

During the development of the HIT MOMENTS product, our development team encountered many difficulties in terms of knowledge and experience. However, thanks to our tireless efforts, we have successfully completed the tasks of each member, thereby helping the product to be completed on time.
